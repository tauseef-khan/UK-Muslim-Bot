*Prayer times:*
- The prayer times used are from East London Mosque. The prayer times will be updated on a yearly basis at the start of each new calendar year. 
- Click [here](http://www.eastlondonmosque.org.uk/sites/default/files/documents/ELM_prayer_timetable_2018.pdf) for the prayer times for 2018.

*Masjid data:*

- The masjid data is taken from the Muslims in Britain website. It will be updated on a montly basis on the 1st of each month. 
- Click [here](http://mosques.muslimsinbritain.org/gps.php) and then click on the 'mosque image' link to download the full dataset.
